Friendsgiving by Folgers®
Friendsgiving is the perfect excuse to gather everyone together during the busy holiday season. Here are a few quick tips for a successful Friendsgiving celebration:
  1. Have everyone bring their favorite Thanksgiving dish. It’s the perfect way to blend old and new traditions.
  2. Keep a few board games on hand for some post-Friendsgiving meal fun.
  3. Make everyone feel extra welcome by having an assortment of Folgers® K-Cup® Pod varieties on hand like Coffeehouse Blend, Caramel Drizzle® and sweet Vanilla Biscotti flavored coffees.
What are your Friendsgiving traditions? Share with us using #Friendsgiving #Folgers.
